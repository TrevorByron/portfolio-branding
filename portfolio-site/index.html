My Background

international/creative/social science/music

Trevor Byron is an Austin based UI/UX designer. Trevor was born in Nairobi, Kenya and spent his growing up years in a remote Maasai village in the southern highlands called the Loita Hills. Trevor's introduction to color and design was learning traditional bead work, making necklaces and bracelets with his "koko" (Maasai Grandma). Trevor designed his first website in 2005 for a punkrock band he was a part of. For kicks, you can check out the old screenshots here! After graduating from Westmont College with a degree in Social Science Trevor dove into creative work, making videos, designing logos and hacking together squarespace websites for artists and non-profits. He also apprenticed as a highend wood worker doing Finish Carpentery in Santa Barbara, CA. As his creative experiences developed he started honing in on his design skills and in early 2017, Trevor graduted form and intensive 3 month training in UI Design from the Iron Yard Acedemy in Austin. Have a look around this site to get a feel for some of the work Trevor has done over the past few years!
