Bucket Budget App

An app that allows users to use the David Ramsey Envelope Budget system without having to carry around actual envelopes of cash

The Problem

A few months ago my wife and I began pulling our monthly budget out in cash, dividing the money into seperate envelopes marked "groceries" "gas" etc. The issues we ran into was often forgeting to bring the envelopes to the store or one person having the envelope when the other needs it.

My Solution

The Bucket Budget app solves this by allowing users to set up a monthly budget by creating seperate "buckets" that when selected money will be drawn from that bucket alone. Linked to your bank account it allows couples with a shared account to use the app at the same time. Following the Dave Ramsey Envelope method this allows users to see the limits of their accounts and regain controll of their spending.

Research

In researching the redesign I interviewed ten Craiglist users and asked them to name three things they liked about their experience with Craigslist and three things that they did not like. They liked that craigslist is to the point without added fluff in its design. They felt that the UX flow is very practical and easy to navigate. And, they liked that there is no clutter from outside adds. What they didn't like was the lack of visual hiarchy, they felt overwhelmed by the amount of text and that the site doesnt work well on mobile.


Wireframes

As we worked out the new wireframes for the site some of the major changes included moving the search bar to the center of the screen and planning out how the site would respond to different sized screens.
